Portrait

Delilah Dominguez

"I enjoy exploring with colors relative to my own emotions, as it helps me create a stronger understanding of myself and my environment. My use of portraiture stems from my own imaginative view of facial characteristics and how they have always been a significant factor in my general experiences To add on to those unrealistic features, using dramatic lines and shapes creates a new perspective to the certainly ambiguous emotions I feel when studying the human features."
