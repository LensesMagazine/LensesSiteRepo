Awaiting 

Carolina Minguez

Genre

Genre

Genre

Oh how I chuckle to such a silly phrase
“I’m not ready for love”
I simply sit
And ask for guidance from those above

Why does a phrase as such linger about
For Juliet was never “ready” for Romeo and the adoration he presented
They just were
Oh how I wish a phrase as this was never invented

I soon await the love I deserve
A love so epic and full of wonders
For the moon and its stars would weep
And those tears would fill beautiful rivers

So astonishing bright
The sun would know no way
And Aphrodite herself
Wouldn’t know what to stay

For the mountains wouldn't look so tall
As we sit together above it all

Till such a glorious day as such
I sit betwixt this evergreen tree
And simply ponder
What an epic love she will be
