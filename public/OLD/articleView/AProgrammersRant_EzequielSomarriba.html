so i'm writing a blog now

Lenses is intended to be a very transparent organization. I think we've fulfilled that intention well. But I'm not telling you about the site to be transparent. I'm telling you about the site because I think it's really interesting.

I'm not going to bore you with technical details, I mean, what kind of writer would I be to knowingly bore my audience? Then again, I'm assuming I'll have an audience...

No, I just want to tell you about what it's like to be the one who (almost) single-handedly sets up the webserver, webpages, layouts, designs, descriptions (and a couple other things), for a completely new magazine that no one might ever read. Hasn't exactly been a dull ride, you see.

Enough ranting.

I want to congratulate a couple of people:
Pat,
Carolina,
Unknowiin404,
Nicole, Leandro, Hans, and Emilio,
and definitely CXPHER!


There are the very special people who've generously donated their works to the film and music section! See, here's the funny thing about accepting essentially any art form that can be digitized. Turns out that a lot of people love writing. A lot of people love drawing and painting. That's cool. That's really cool. (I mean, seriously, have you seen the beauties people have made?) But it took the musicians a minute to open up. Honestly, I was scared. I think the entire board was. Starting something up from scratch like this is a HUGE risk - it's impossible to know just how many people might eventually enjoy this magazine. It's why we were scared that we might never receive any music or film pieces. But, thanks to a bit of harassment advertisement and lobbying, CXPHER and pat came to our rescue. So, thank you! Seriously. You rescued me (and probably the rest of the board) from a lot of anxiety.

By the way, I'm sorry to anyone who tried to watch Dinglepop during the past couple of days. It has been having issues loading correctly, and it was kind of a complicated issue to fix, but it's working now! Better late than never, right? Anyways, you definitely should go watch that. I mean, I love that you've made it this far into my own post, but I swear, you'd be missing out to leave this site without watching that film. It makes me feel like Halloween isn't over yet, and I love it. I'll put another link to it at the end of the post.

Then again, you should probably just go listen to every song we have on here. Pat's "mend me" is still my all-time favorite film/music submission on here. I swear, some of these singers don't even sound like high schoolers. It's like I'm listening to Billie Eilish's songs before she became a professional musician or something. Seriously, I really believe that all these people really could have their work cut out for them if they went into the industry one day. Never give up on your dreams, guys!



I have an idea.

There's A LOT that I can talk about regarding Lenses. (Or my personal life, for that matter.) So, what do you want to hear? Seriously. AMA.



Before you go, I did promise you a link: go watch dinglepop!
Thank you for using Lenses. I'm really happy that people are actually enjoying this project we've all put so much work into.
