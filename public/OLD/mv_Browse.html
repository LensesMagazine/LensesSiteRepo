Moog Runner

​Agustin Martinez

"The name behind the song refers to one of the most influential and prominent synthesizers, the Moog synthesizer. Created in 1964, this song is a kind of ode to the influence and impact that this sound had in the last 60 years and "running" by emulating similar sounds of a Moog synthesizer. The name also drives inspiration from the arpeggiated synth that seems to "run" hence the name of "Running Moog"."
