Craters on My Skin 

Isabel Marie

Genre

Genre

Genre

The thought invades my head

Silently immersing itself in the swirls of confusion that have left my mind vulnerable

I acknowledge its existence...
I start waving it off

But before I dismiss it my reason has abandoned me.
Whatever it is I was standing on crumbles under my feet,
So I start to make momentary excuses,
Justifying the impulse and desire that bleeds over what they tell me is common sense
Until a small corner of mymindthatgoesfranticindistress, silences

I look down...
My eyes trail from the lines on my skin to the sharp edge at my fingertips.
I watch it move closer...and closer...
The blade settles, hovering over, filling me with anticipation
I feel a subtle familiar sting as it quickly glides through making a path

I wait a short moment...
Long enough to wonder if my skins turned to brick,
as a way to save the trouble and fatigue of repairing itself.

After barely having entertained the idea, I see an opening wound...
Coloring itself the rare shade of red we're not supposed to see

Liquified ruby droplets emerge from beneath.
Exhilaration spreads through me
They pool together and I watch in a trance...
Hypnotized...
as they melt down the sides of my arm,
Tainting where they run through...
More and more start to bleed out of me

I'm snapped out of my daze and hurriedly wash clean,
Attempting to contain more streams of stunning red than I expected to have found their way out

They stop eventually.

The moment passes

The forbidden sight and accompanying feeling start to fade

I try to grasp it again

1 more. then 3...

My head calms
the haze clears
I count them
that's 20 in the past couple days

I walk out, open the door to the class, and sit in my chair
All emotion, longing, desperateness, thirst, confusion, stress
The high subsides in a wisp signifying temporary closure

I sit in my chair listening to the lecture and go through the motions
The craters on my skin, bittersweet reminders
Knowing i'll only last until the same thought entices me again into guilty indulgence
