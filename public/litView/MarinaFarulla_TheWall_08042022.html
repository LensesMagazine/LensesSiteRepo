The Wall 

Marina Farulla

Genre

Genre

Genre


They say we are our worst
critics,
enemies.
But I was hers.
Day after day,
I spoke to a wall.
A wall that prioritized its
structure,
at the expense of each,
individual,
brick.
Now dressed in a series of
cracks,
holes,
dents.
