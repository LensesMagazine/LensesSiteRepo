29/11/20

     It’s been quite a while since I’ve paid enough attention or found the courage in my long rotten heart to send you a message. I’ve been focusing on some work; just the things that tore us apart. It’s quite melodramatic; the overindulgence, the overworking, the such; things that you could’ve seen me overcome.
     In work I’ve joined a company and I work in a very average office space with very average people, many see it boring, but I learned to appreciate menial details. I’m enamored by my coworker’s daily news, their children’s achievements, the office birthdays, the bland, the grey, and the particularly silent; it’s extremely romantic. On the topic of actual work, I’m entirely uninterested in what I actually do on the daily basis all I actually do or have done so far has been checking little boxes on a long list of qualities I have to observe on particular clients files; this type of work has never sparked any emotion in me.
     I miss the misty soft beige fields of home; the city hasn’t interested me, the grid, the grey cubes, the simpleness of it all, the cafes don’t allow you to stay for too long, everything Is quite aggressive. I’ve never been very outgoing or too establishing, you know that- but I still try to be so; everyone is so aggressive, and it doesn’t hurt half as much as being sunk or stoned. Being tied to a horse fully nude covered in the most oppressive chains ever forged would be preferable to the dismissal I face. I’m not being abused by others, or at least it isn’t as active as some physical hazing. I’m always being dismissed.
     Otherwise, hope is very close and I feel as if life will get better for me.


-lovingly your own

29/11/22

     A heartfelt message wouldn’t summarize my feelings, some menial mistake I’ve made has taken control of my life. I write to you because I’ve been ignoring your messages, more actively I haven’t given them the mind they claim And deserve.
     I write to you not to pity, treacle me in your apathy; the apathy I’ve accustomed myself to, or to beg for forgiveness. You were right, always right; I surrender myself to you, at least anything that has been left.
     The first few months of my work I’ve felt happy but constantly in a social atmosphere I; oppressed in speech, couldn’t speak my mind or tell people exactly what I thought of them, the romance of simple life was beguiling; so I quickly loathed it and distanced myself from a simple modest life. My choice hasn’t been well on my health as the atmosphere trampled me.
     I miss you, only you, the one thing that is entirely real; yet I still don’t know you at all. All you are to me is a mysterious reflection; any detail in your reflection is missing and disorienting, I look at your eyes and nothing stare back, I focus on your nose: nothing sparks emotion, I look at your roguish lips: and it only speaks in a hollow language which seizes truth.
     You are evil. You’re the city, the grid, the grey, my romance, the menial, the painfully average, my inquisitor, you espouse the wickedness of the average; the voyeur demon who throws darts in women’s eyes. Everything that is wicked is your disciple. Evil is produced in your image.
     All you seek in me is yourself; you shouldn’t tempt me anymore or seek me out; I’ve given up on everything, my modest life, my job, the bland food I eat, and the social events I pretend I love. I despise it all. Everything you are, is me. I am accattone, the scum of society that just gets by, the most blind, servants to nonage, and lovers of disappointment. Everything I have done has been to serve you when I should have been living for myself.
Hope is close and I feel as if my life will get better
