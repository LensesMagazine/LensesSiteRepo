Cristian Diaz

skys falling when the sun cries
rain calling as the water rise
soul rotting when I compromise
been a cold comet since my fire died

no sonnet these are written lies
my bondage keep me tied to life
its unconscious so I ride the tide
somewhere when I tried to find

a moment to submit to myself
a core component that composes our realm
I sought seclusion as a student to repel
a wounded contusion alluding to illusions of hell

promised u that I’d fight through my plight
golden ratio , a woeful radio where silence wields the foresight of my height,
it’s more time than life
it’s just me in my mind
it’s more time than life
can’t you feel what’s right

my opponent I oppose my own companion
soul unfolding as construction to my pantheon
battle boredom i beget my own battalion
paddle forward In the waters of Atlanteans

I fill my filaments to form lore like a paladin
pain is the currency of being compassionate
I envelop my soul in yours till entanglement
affected from amends amidst abandonment

I eject freedom from fear for I’m cantankerous
feeling foreign to myself is solely maddening
overheard you yelling from years pledged to avarice
lying for hope is like cloaking a mortal wound with bandages


cumulonimbus the area that inches hysteria of pigments for various limits on the territory of my inner richness a terraforming sickness impaired in my despair as I divulge from my addictions address me with conviction for I dress my mind with decorations celebrating marvelous conditions .

dishonest, my
heart rocky
regardless, I’ll
keep walking
blithe dread ,
I’m bloody haunted ,
my passions unrequited ,
I bled out my head for I knew what a bond is.

I’m giving you my misery,
I relinquish my addiction to be spirited literally,
existence is disheartening,
you can’t measure my conscience,
a measly miniature makeshift artist,
I find no strength in armaments,
I walk with death at length so my depth is bottomless,
my stress is death a defeaning regress of amenable arguments,
what’s left when I etch nonsense from the nest of my consciousness ?

does my energy go into the fiction of my lines or the reality of my being?
are my lines benign in sorcery or worthy of believing ?
have I mastered the art of deceiving ?
or am I missing the causality of reason ?
