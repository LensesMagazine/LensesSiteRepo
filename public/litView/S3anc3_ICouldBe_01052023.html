I Could Be 

S3anc3

Genre

Genre

Genre

I could be:

I could be your muse.
Somebody that for the “sake of art”,
You can use.
And I’d let you.
Hell, I’d beg you.

I could be your midnight love.
The one you call when you can’t sleep,
So we never hang up.
You don’t have to pretend that you know me during the daylight.
Because I won’t care as long as I’m your nightlife.
And I’d let you.
Hell, I’d beg you.

It could be 3 AM, speaking in a voice I’d miss the moment I wake up, because I don’t know if I’ll hear you that way again.
We could be talking about life or playing truth or dare and we could lay it all out there because no one will care!
And then I could pine and write and pine some more.
Rinse and repeat until I can’t pine anymore.

Of course that won’t happen.
It’s 3 PM not AM and I am no muse.
Yes I’ll pine ‘til I die, ‘cause it’s no use.
I want to be it all.
But I know I couldn’t be any.
I want to be on that balcony, that bike, be in songs and poems that you write.
I’d let you.
God- can’t you see me begging you?

I could be your time-capsule.
I’ll remember everything that we share.
That way, you don’t have to, which seems fair.
I want it in my head to think about at night,
You want to be free, so you hand the memories over, no fight.

Do you remember when we met?
Do you remember what you asked?
That moment after school, of which since a couple months have past.
Who? You asked.
You dared.
And for the first time since we met, I think I noticed that maybe you also care.
I too could have the courage to dare.
After all, I always cared.

I loved you then
And I love you still.
Part of me knows deep down
That I always will.

I could be your muse.
I could be a hopeless romantic who will fuel your painfully beautiful soul.
Your excruciatingly brilliant mind.
Your addictive and heartbreaking words.
Your enchanting and tender eyes…

I could be all that you ask of me.
Yes. I could be…
