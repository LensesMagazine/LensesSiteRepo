Welcome to Lenses!

We are Lenses Literary and Arts Magazine, a magazine (both digital and physical) managed by Doral Academy students for Doral Academy students. Our mission is to provide a unique platform for showcasing the creative talents of our peers. With us, young artists of all kinds gain the opportunity to have their work featured, receive recognition, and build a portfolio of published art.
