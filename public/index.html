Welcome to Lenses!

Visual ArtsMusic + VideoLiterature

Welcome to Lenses, the literature and arts club. Our focus is exhibiting student-made works of art, including anything from visual arts, film, performing arts, music, or any other form of art. We believe every artist deserves to have a place where they can freely share their art for the world to see and enjoy. On this site, you can find galleries for any kind of art, along with a link below to upload your own piece too!

Lenses relies on student artists (like you!) so that we can continue to inspire other artists and show the world what the students at Doral can do. We appreciate every single one of our artists who contributes to the site, and we will continue to treat every piece with the upmost respect.
